A Hanjie puzzle consists of a rectangular grid with one or more clues for each row and column of the puzzle. Clues for each row are at the left of the puzzle; clues for columns are at the top. Each set of clues tells you about the shaded squares in its row or column. hese sets of clues are all that you need to solve the entire puzzle. Rows or columns with only a single number clue reveal that there are that many consecutive shaded squares somewhere in that row/column. Other squares in the row/column must be empty.

An explanation of game strategies can be found by clicking here

Click a cell to toggle it from unmarked to marked are simple placeholders. Grey cells are marked cells, white cells are unmarked cells

When you click to toggle a box, you can also drag the mouse to draw the same type of box on multiple cells.

You can hold Shift Key and click or drag the mouse to unmark cells

You can also press Show Solution to reveal solution or Reset to reset the game.

Lastly, you can click start to record total time to solve the puzzle
